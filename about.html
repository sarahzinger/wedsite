Timeline



Aaron and Sarah

April 10th, 2011
We met the same way all true nerds meet--with the help of a good algorithm. Thanks to OkCupid, we arranged to meet at the Metro Diner on the Upper West Side to enjoy some brunch and went on to explore a comic book festival downtown.

Summer, 2011
Sarah decided that it would be a good idea to teach tennis and reading to youngsters in Boston for the summer. Aaron decides to visit and doesn't end up leaving till a month later.

September, 2011
After accidentally living together in Boston's smallest room imaginable, Aaron and Sarah decide to share a slightly larger studio in Harlem. Together they build many happy memories despite a leaky ceiling, rat-infested walls, and loud neighbors.

Spring and Summer 2012
Aaron gets his current job at Medidata and Sarah graduates from Hunter. We decided to book it for Brooklyn for a smaller but more functional apartment in Fort Greene with a great view. Sarah also gets her first real job.

2012-2014
Sarah tries a few different social work jobs before realizing that she'd rather be a programmer like Aaron. Aaron graciously tries to teach Sarah everything he knows. In the meantime, we decide to start a new organization called Americans Give in a vain attempt to save the world, which becomes one of many side projects we hope to accomplish together. We also begin to review Star Trek on a weekly basis, much to the chagrin of everyone's facebook newsfeeds.

Summer 2014
Shortly before her 26th birthday, Aaron proposed to Sarah during one of their weekly Star Trek reviews. The ring, a ruby, stands as a reminder of the computer programming language Aaron has been teaching Sarah in their free time. #nerds.

September 5, 2015
We get married! 0th wedding anniversary.
